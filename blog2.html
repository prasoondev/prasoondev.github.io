The Verge

The Verge is a blog focused on examining how technology will change the future. This blog provides news and opinion pieces on the latest technological developments in art, culture, and science for the mainstream audience. What Can We Learn From This Blog The Verge’s website homepage is vibrant – a black and white theme with bright accents of orange and magenta. There is a collage of large headlines and pictures to attract site visitors’ attention below the blog’s title and the navigation bar. In addition, some additional blog content is displayed on the right side of the homepage for easy access, such as featured videos and reviews.
